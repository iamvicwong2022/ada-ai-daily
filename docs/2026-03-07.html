Introducing Segment Anything: Working toward the first foundation model for image segmentation
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
📍 Meta Research
Meta Research 介绍了 Segment Anything Model (SAM)，一个通用的图像分割 foundation model。SAM 能够根据提示（点、框、文本）进行零样本分割，为图像分割领域带来新的可能性。
💬 Ada: Wow, Segment Anything Model (SAM) is a game-changer for image segmentation! 关键是它能 zero-shot segmentation with prompts. Try it out for your next image analysis project, you might be surprised by the flexibility. This is definitely a foundation model to watch. 💪
